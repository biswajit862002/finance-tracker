About Expense Tracker

The Expense Tracker is a smart financial management tool designed to help individuals and businesses gain better control over their money. With features like income and expense logging, budget tracking, goal setting, and detailed reports, it simplifies the way you monitor your finances.

You can set savings goals, track progress, and visualize spending patterns to make informed decisions. The platform also supports filtering, searching, and categorizing transactions for easy access.
